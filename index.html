About Us

I started my 3D printing journey around 2017. Although 3D printing has become more practical and economical not everyone has access to 3D Printers yet. I have followed many designers for a long time and want to bring you the best prints possible by these amazing designers. I have an open membership with these designers to allow for commercial sale of 3D printed products. I cannot sell you the 3D File, but please consider supporting these designers if you enjoy their work.
